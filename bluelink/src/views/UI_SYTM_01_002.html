  • Action plan (EN)
    • 1. Remove all BEQ connector (24V, GND, 12V) with engine-OFF → Check whether normal operation is performed after recombination
    • 2. Check for short circuit between 24V-GND cables
    • 3. Check for short circuit between 12V-GND cables
    • 4. Check whether it is normal after replacing the unit (if there are no abnormalities above)

    • 12V-GND 케이블 간 쇼트 여부 점검
